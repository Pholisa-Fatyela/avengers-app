Avengers

The Avengers are a team of superheroes in the Marvel Comics universe. They are known for their bravery, strength, and ability to work together to overcome formidable foes. The Avengers were formed to combat threats that no single hero could withstand. Their roster has included iconic characters such as Iron Man, Thor, Captain America, and Black Widow.

Iron Man

Genius inventor and billionaire Tony Stark fights evil in his advanced suit of armor.

Thor

The Norse God of Thunder wields his mighty hammer Mjolnir to protect Earth and Asgard.

Captain America

Steve Rogers, a super-soldier from World War II, stands for justice and freedom.

Black Widow

Natasha Romanoff is a master spy and martial artist, known for her intelligence and agility.

Hulk

Dr. Bruce Banner transforms into the powerful Hulk when angered, smashing obstacles in his way.

Hawkeye

Clint Barton is a master archer with incredible accuracy and combat skills.
